Vision

A strong institution and catalyst for the provision of adequate and affordable housing, inclusive human settlements and well-planned communities for every Filipino family.

Mission

To improve the lives of the Filipino people as the primary government agency in the management of housing, human settlements and urban development.

Core Values

DHSUD — D.E.H.S.U.

Dynamism Honesty Service Excellence Unity of Purpose Education

Our Mandate

1. Primary national government entity for housing, human settlements and urban development.

2. Sole planning, policy-making, regulatory, and performance monitoring entity.

  • Land use planning and monitoring
  • Regulatory function over subdivisions
  • Registration and supervision of HOAs

3. Develop national strategy for affordable housing.
